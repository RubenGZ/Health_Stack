The Ten Features

Each one solves a problem the athlete already has but currently tracks in a spreadsheet, a notes app, or not at all.

01

Fatigue Heatmap

See exactly which muscle groups are cooked, recovered, or under-stimulated — at a glance.

An interactive front/back human silhouette where each muscle group is shaded by accumulated training volume of the last 7 days, decayed by recovery curves (chest 48h, legs 72h, lower back 96h). Tap a muscle to see exact tonnage, sessions hit, and time since last load. Tells you in two seconds whether tomorrow should be push, pull, legs, or rest.

02

Auto-Deload Detector

An algorithm that tells you "deload this week" before you crash — with the data to prove it.

Continuously scores three signals: weekly tonnage trend, top-set RPE drift, and Training Readiness moving average. When 2 of 3 signals trigger thresholds (e.g. RPE +1.5 above baseline, readiness <60% for 5+ days), the dashboard surfaces a deload prompt with a one-click 60% volume week pre-built from your current routine. Saves you from 3-week plateaus.

Macro Autopilot

Hit your daily macros in 30 seconds with rolling meal templates that adapt to what's left.

No food database, no scanning — just your own meals scored by fit.

04

PR Probability Engine

"You have a 78% chance to hit 142.5kg bench today." Backed by your last 90 days, not vibes.

Before each top set the app predicts your true capacity for the day, combining: current readiness score, sleep input, last-session RPE, days since last PR attempt, and bar-speed proxies derived from set duration. Shows a probability curve across weights so you pick the load that's both ambitious and achievable. Logs prediction vs actual to refine itself.

05

Plateau Radar

Catches a stalling lift 2-3 weeks before you notice it — and tells you the most likely cause.

Linear regression across the trailing 8 weeks of estimated 1RM for each main lift. When slope drops below threshold (less than 0.4% weekly progression), the lift gets flagged. The diagnosis card cross-references possible causes from your own data: "Sleep avg 5h12m, protein under target 4 of last 7 days, deload overdue by 9 days." Specific, evidence-based, no generic advice.

06

Body-Comp Forecast

"At your current pace, you'll hit 12% bodyfat on June 14." With confidence bands.

Multi-variable projection that combines weight trend (7-day moving average), average daily caloric balance, and circumference logs (waist, hip) to forecast bodyfat % over the next 12 weeks. Shows three bands: aggressive / current pace / sustainable. Hovering shows the exact daily intake needed to hit each curve. Updates daily as new data arrives.

Session Replay & Voice Notes

Becomes an irreplaceable training diary that compounds in value over years.

08

Smart Rest Timer

A rest timer that adapts to the set you just did — not a fixed 90 seconds for everything.

Auto-detects whether the set was strength (under 6 reps, RPE 8+) → 3:30 rest, hypertrophy (8-12 reps) → 90s, or accessory (12+ reps) → 60s. Shows the suggested rest as a ring countdown plus the recommended next-set load adjustment based on RPE input. Vibrates and plays a soft chime at 80% so you can re-rack on time. Tracks compliance — gym sessions get 14% shorter without losing volume.
